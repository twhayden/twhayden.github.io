Keynote Address

John Egazarian

Find a Way to Win: Everything You Wanted to Know about Launching a Startup but Were Afraid to Ask

11:00am at Gundlach Theatre

John Egazarian most recently served as Chief Operation Officer at CUR Media Inc., a startup business in the streaming music market. He was responsible for all aspects of the company's internal operations and directed the build and launch of the CUR Music application for both mobile and web users.

John has over 20 years of experience leading the delivery of innovation software initiatives in complex, competitive environments. Prior to joining CUR Media, John led eBusiness programs at Fallon Community Health, Travelers Insurance, and WellPoint. He started his career as a consultant at Arthur Anderson. John is a graduate of the University of Connecticut. He lives in Marlborough, Connecticut with his wife, Cheryl, and their three daughters.
